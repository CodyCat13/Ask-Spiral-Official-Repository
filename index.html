They may be motivated to work for it or something. If one asks a question and receives a negative prediction (like 4 and below), they may unknowingly bring about that result because they believe so. This phenomenon doesn't affect skeptics since they don't believe in stuff relating to advanced psychic energy at all, but please do not take Spiral's predictions too seriously (unless you want to I guess).
